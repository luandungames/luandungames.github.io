This Privacy Policy explains how information about you is collected, used, and disclosed by Luandun Games Pte. Collection of Information

Information You Provide to Luandun Games. Luandun Games collects information you provide directly to it, such as if you register for a Service; participate in Luandun Games contests and special events; request technical support; or send Luandun Games an email or otherwise interact with it. The types of information Luandun Games may collect directly from you include your name, e-mail address, and phone number.

Information Luandun Games Collects Automatically. When you access the Services, Luandun Games automatically collects certain information, including:

  • Log Information: Luandun Games logs information about your use of the Services, including the type of browser you use, access times, pages viewed, your IP address and the page you visited before navigating to its websites.
  • Device Information: When you access or use the Services, Luandun Games collects information about (i) device properties, including, but not limited to Media Access Control (“MAC”) address, unique device identifier (“UDID”), or other device identifiers; (ii) device software platform and firmware; (iii) mobile phone carrier; (iv) geographical data such as zip code, area code and location; and (v) game score and achievements. Luandun Games may also collect other data as reasonably required by Luandun Games to provide, maintain and improve the Services.

Information Collected by Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies: Luandun Games may use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to collect information about you and your interaction with the Services. We use this information in one or more of the ways described in the “Use of Information” section below. Luandun Games may also collect information using web beacons or other tracking technologies to help deliver cookies, count visits, and understand usage and campaign effectiveness. For more information about cookies, please see “Your Privacy Choices” below.

Information Luandun Games Collects from Other Sources. Luandun Games may receive information about you from other sources and combine or link that with information it has about you. For example, when you authorize Luandun Games to connect to certain third-party social networking sites, Luandun Games will access information about you from those sites, such as your name, account information, friend lists, and any other information you make publicly available. Luandun Games collects this information in accordance with the privacy policies and authorization procedures determined by third-party social networking sites in addition to the privacy settings you establish with those sites. For more information, please refer directly to the privacy policies of the relevant third-party social networking sites.
